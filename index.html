What we cover

Four kinds of cover that guard your life

Buying life insurance isn't just picking the lowest price. We help you understand the options, how they protect you, and why you might need each one.

Life Insurance

If the worst happens, who looks after your partner and your kids? A lump sum can pay off the mortgage and keep your family in their home — and keep your plans for them alive.

TPD Insurance

If you're injured and can never work again, how do you keep providing? Total & Permanent Disability cover clears your debts and funds the transition to a very different future.

Trauma Insurance

A cancer, heart attack or stroke diagnosis can cost thousands out of pocket — even with Medicare. Trauma cover pays a lump sum so you can focus on recovery, not the bills.

Income Protection

Can't work, can't earn — so who pays the mortgage? Income Protection replaces up to 70% of your income after a waiting period, so you can focus on your health, not your finances.

Life, TPD and Income Protection can be funded through your super. When you've just taken on a mortgage and cashflow is tight, that means staying protected with no hit to your take-home pay.
